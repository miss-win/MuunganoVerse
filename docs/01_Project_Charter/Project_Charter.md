## Project Background

The United Republic of Tanzania has made significant efforts to promote Union education through schools, public awareness campaigns, publications, museums, media programmes, and digital initiatives. As technology continues to transform how people access information, there is an opportunity to complement these efforts through an interactive digital ecosystem that enhances participation, accessibility, and lifelong learning.

MuunganoVerse is proposed as a modern digital platform that combines education, storytelling, gamification, community participation, and heritage preservation into one engaging experience.

---

## Problem Statement

Existing Union education initiatives provide valuable information, but today's learners—especially young people—increasingly prefer interactive, mobile, and engaging digital experiences. Educational resources are also distributed across multiple channels, creating an opportunity for a unified platform that improves access, participation, and collaboration while preserving Union heritage.
